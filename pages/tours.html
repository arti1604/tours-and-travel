Special Interest Tour Packages

Tour My India offers Special Interest Tours for travellers who want to experience something
based on their special interest. These are tours that are different from the usual touristy stuff &
focus on a particular activity for which a particular region or place is famous. From the joy of tea tasting to taking a tribal tour of Central India, Gujarat & Northeast India, you can plan a tour based on the specific interes you have in mind. Our expertly created Special Interest Tours also include photography, angling & fishing, wine tasting, snake, spice plantation, butterfly & flower tours.
