India

India, a vast and diverse country in South Asia, is known for its rich cultural heritage, historic landmarks, and vibrant traditions. From the Himalayan mountains to its bustling cities and serene beaches, it offers a unique blend of ancient history and modern development.

Paris, the capital of France, is celebrated for its world-renowned landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. Known as the "City of Light," it is famed for its art, fashion, and romantic ambiance.
