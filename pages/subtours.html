Delhi - Mandawa (260 km)

After breakfast leave for Bikaner. On arrival, visit the royal walled medieval city dating back to 1486 AD and established by
Prince Bika Rathore. Bikaner was a great center for trade in the ancient world as it was between the ancient caravan route,
which linked Central Asia and North India with Gujarat seaports. We will be visiting the massive fort of Junagarh built betw
een 1588 - 1593 AD by Raja Rai Singh (1571 - 1611 AD). Junagarh fort is an architectural masterpiece. Further, we will visit
the Lalgarh palace made by red sandstone and built by Maharaja Ganga Singh (1881 AD - 1942 AD) in memory of his father
Maharaja Lal Singh between 1902 -1926. Lalgarh is an example of pure Rajput martial architecture. Later, proceed to Gajner.
On arrival, check into the hotel for an overnight stay.

Jaisalmer - Sam (45 km)

After breakfast proceed for city sightseeing tour of Jaisalmer and visit the Jaisalmer fort, through the narrows streets dotted
with temples & havelis. Further, visit Gadisar Lake, famous havelis and Bada Bagh. In the evening return back to Sam. Enjoy
camel ride at the dunes. Enjoy cultural performance followed by dinner in Sam. An overnight in Sam Further, we will visit the
village of Bawari Kallan. Here, we will have a walk through the streets of the vill age to observe the typical rural life of the
ers Further, continue drive to Jaisalmer. On arrival, check into the hotel for a night stay..
